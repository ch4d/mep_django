Tutoring

The MEP tutoring lab offers assistance in engineering, math and science courses. The tutors are trained to assist you learn the material based on your learning style so you can excel on your exams and homework on your own. The lab is open during the fall, and spring semesters from the second week of the semester through dead weak. Take a look at the schedule to see if the course you would need tutoring in is available.
