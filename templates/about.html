Why We Are Here
Everything has a purpose.

Half the students in the country who select engineering or computer science majors are no longer in these majors after their first science course. This is not because they are not capable or well prepared. It is because they started a long and complicated journey without a map or guide. Our purpose is to provide this roadmap along with opportunities and a way for students to earn workplace experience, learn about scholarships exclusive to engineering and computer science students, and talk to alumni who have been in their shoes before.
